\section{Context}

Collision avoidance in orbit is the archetypal dual-stream critical-system loop. Noisy open catalogs and amateur observations vs.\ high-trust classified radars. An operator in the loop before any maneuver. Confidence thresholds that trigger money (delta-v) and avoid Kessler cascades.

\section{Cortices}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.2em]
  \item \texttt{catalog} --- TLE / ephemeris ingestion, orbital propagation
  \item \texttt{observations} --- radar + optical tracking data normalization
  \item \texttt{conjunction-analysis} --- close-approach screening with confidence bands
  \item \texttt{correlation} --- dual-stream fusion (public catalog $\times$ classified radar tracks)
  \item \texttt{maneuver-planning} --- burn windows, delta-v budget, post-maneuver conjunction re-check
\end{itemize}

\section{Entity model}

\begin{figure}[H]
  \centering
  \includegraphics[width=0.9\linewidth]{figures/06-entity-model.pdf}
  \caption{Satellite / Debris / Observation / ConjunctionEvent / Maneuver. Every maneuver is reversible-by-design through the audit ledger.}
  \label{fig:arch-06-entities}
\end{figure}

\textbf{HITL = mission operator.} Every \texttt{ConjunctionEvent} above threshold ($P \geq 10^{-4}$, standard NASA convention) becomes a Sweep finding. The operator validates or rejects before any burn is committed. Audit row per decision --- the \texttt{Maneuver} ledger is reversible-by-design (a burn can be reconstructed from the audit trail post-incident).

\textbf{Why a platform, not a product:} any org running space assets --- operators, agencies, earth-observation primes, secure-comms providers --- has a variant of this loop. The platform industrializes it once (orchestrator, swarm, guardrails, HITL, audit); each tenant plugs its own catalog sources, radar feeds, and thresholds behind the same \texttt{SourceFetcher} interface.
