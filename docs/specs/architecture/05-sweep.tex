\section{What the swarm looks for}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.2em]
  \item \textbf{Inconsistencies} --- missing fields, suspect classifications, stale entries. Scanned in parallel, rate-limited, budgeted.
  \item \textbf{Catalog enrichment} (April 2026) --- two fill paths emitting navigable findings in the knowledge graph:
  \begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.2em]
    \item \emph{Web mission} (nano + \texttt{web\_search}) --- structured-outputs JSON schema, hedging-token blocklist, source-URL validation, per-column range guards, unit-mismatch check, 2-vote corroboration. Per-satellite granularity, payload-only filter.
    \item \emph{KNN propagation} (zero-LLM) --- for each payload missing a field, finds $K$ nearest embedded neighbours (Voyage halfvec cosine) with the field set and propagates their consensus value. $\pm 10\%$ agreement on numeric, 2/3 mode coverage on text.
    \item Every fill emits a \texttt{research\_finding} (\texttt{cortex=data\_auditor}, \texttt{finding\_type=insight}) with \texttt{research\_edge}s --- \texttt{about} $\to$ target, \texttt{similar\_to} $\to$ neighbours / cited URL.
  \end{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Orbital reflexion pass} --- second-pass anomaly detector that cross-tabulates a suspect satellite's orbital fingerprint (inclination, RAAN, mean motion) against the declared classification. Surfaces military-lineage peers (Yaogan, Cosmos, NROL, Shiyan\dots) sharing the same inclination belt. Emits \texttt{anomaly} findings with navigable provenance. Pure SQL, no LLM.
  \item \textbf{Editorial copilot} --- same pipeline drafts structured briefings from audited data.
  \item \textbf{Autonomy loop} --- \texttt{POST /api/autonomy/start} rotates Thalamus cycles (6 rotating SSA queries) with Sweep null-scans; topbar pill + FEED panel stream each tick live in the console.
\end{itemize}
